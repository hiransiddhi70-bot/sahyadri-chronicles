Culture & Traditions

Major Festivals of Maharashtra

🪔 Ganesh Chaturthi
Started by Lokmanya Tilak as a public celebration. It honors Lord Ganesha and symbolizes wisdom, prosperity and unity. Celebrated for 10 days with grand processions and cultural events.

🎉 Gudhi Padwa
Maharashtrian New Year celebrated by raising the Gudhi outside homes. It represents victory, prosperity and new beginnings.

🪔 Diwali
Festival of Lights celebrating the victory of good over evil. Homes are decorated with diyas, rangoli and sweets.

🌾 Makar Sankranti
Harvest festival celebrated by flying kites and exchanging Tilgul sweets with the message "Tilgul ghya, god god bola".

💃 Navratri
Nine nights dedicated to Goddess Durga with traditional dances, prayers and cultural celebrations.

Traditional Lifestyle
